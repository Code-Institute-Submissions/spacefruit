Things We Do

Here at Spacefruit we exist to create, and our creativity knows no bounds. We pride ourselves in being a dynamic and innovative creative agency that thrives on blending imagination with strategy and skill.

Branding

At Spacefruit, we understand that your brand is the soul of your business. Our dedicated branding experts dive deep into the ethos, values, and visions of your company to craft a unique identity that resonates. From conceptualizing logo designs to shaping brand narratives, we ensure that your brand stands out.

Design

Design isn't just about aesthetics; it's about communicating a message. Our team brings together form and function to create visually compelling and strategically aligned designs. Whether it's web design, packaging, or print, we merge creativity with purpose, ensuring your business engages and inspires.

Animation

In a world saturated with content, animation can bring your stories to life in dynamic ways. We help breathe life into ideas, crafting engaging and impactful animations that captivate your audience. From intricate 2D animations to immersive 3D experiences, we turn your visions into moving masterpieces.

Our Latest Creations

Explore our portfolio featuring our latest works, where we've crafted captivating brand identities and launched stellar product campaigns that push the boundaries of creativity and innovation.

Silence Is Golden.

Client: Bose

We orchestrated a compelling and immersive campaign for the launch of the newest Bose noise-cancelling headphones on the market. Focusing on the theme 'Silence is Golden,' our objective was to elevate headphones into personal, soundproof havens.

Retro Living.

Client: Ikea

We were thrilled to recently partner with IKEA on the 'Retro Living' campaign, a nostalgic celebration of timeless design. This advert took customers on a vintage voyage, blending modern functionality with classic aesthetics to showcase IKEA's specially curated collection.

Feel The Air.

Client: Nike

Nike challenged us to take on the 'Feel the Air' campaign for their revolutionary new pair of shoes. Designed to transcend the boundaries of footwear, this campaign showcased the sensation of weightlessness and the power of breathability.
